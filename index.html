CLICK ME TO START!

Rules are simple. Click the "CLICK ME TO START" button on top of the page
. Listen and look at a button clicked by the game. You should click the same button.
On the next level the game will click another or the same button and will form a pattern (for example the game clicked Sonic on level 1 and Mario on level 2) and you should replicate the pattern by clicking Sonic from level 1 and Mario on level 2 etc.
Please, try to enjoy the game :D
